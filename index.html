About Slipknot

Slipknot is a Grammy Award-winning heavy metal band from Des Moines, Iowa. Known for their energetic live performances and distinctive image, the band has gained a massive following since its formation in 1995.

Their music often explores themes of chaos, anger, and the human experience, with albums like "Iowa" and "All Hope Is Gone" becoming iconic in the metal genre.

Band Members

  • Corey Taylor: Vocals
  • Mick Thomson: Guitar
  • Jim Root: Guitar
  • Craig Jones: Samples/Keyboards
  • Shawn Crahan: Percussion
  • Jay Weinberg: Drums (now ex-drummer)
  • Alessandro Venturella: Bass
  • Sid Wilson: Turntables
